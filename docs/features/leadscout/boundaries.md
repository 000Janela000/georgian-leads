# LeadScout — Boundaries

> Last updated: 2026-03-21

What LeadScout deliberately does NOT do, and why.

## Does Not Send Messages Automatically

**Reason:** Georgian outreach works best when personal. AI-generated or templated messages are weak in Georgian language and culture. You message each lead yourself on Facebook, in your own words. This is a competitive advantage, not a limitation.

## Does Not Support Multiple Users

**Reason:** This is a personal tool for one person. No authentication, no user accounts, no team features. Adding multi-user support would add complexity with no benefit.

## Does Not Import From Business Registries

**Reason:** Registry data (OpenSanctions, CompanyInfo.ge) shows legally registered companies but tells you nothing about their online activity. Google Maps shows businesses that are actively operating and getting customer reviews — a much better signal for lead quality.

## Does Not Scrape Financial Data

**Reason:** Revenue and profit data from reportal.ge was useful for the registry-based approach. In the Google Maps approach, review count and rating are better proxies for business health and activity. Simpler, free, and more reliable.

## Does Not Target Foreign Markets

**Reason:** Focus. The Georgian market is where you have the language advantage, cultural understanding, and ability to do manual outreach. Foreign markets would require automated email, domain warming, deliverability infrastructure, and English templates — a completely different product. May revisit later.

## Does Not Detect Outdated Websites

**Reason:** Scope control for Phase 1. A business with a bad website is a valid lead (Type C), but detecting "bad" requires analyzing page quality (mobile responsiveness, SSL, design age). This is planned for Phase 3 if needed.

## Does Not Provide CRM Features

**Reason:** LeadScout tracks lead status (New through Won) and nothing more. It does not manage deals, invoices, contracts, or client relationships. Use a separate tool if you need full CRM functionality.

## Does Not Categorize Businesses Automatically

**Reason:** Google Maps categories are often vague ("Local Business") or wrong. LeadScout shows whatever category Google Maps provides as a label, but does not filter or sort by category. Your targeting is based on activity signals (reviews, Facebook presence), not business type.

## Does Not Work Offline

**Reason:** Discovery requires Google Places API and Google CSE API calls. Facebook enrichment requires internet access. The lead list and tracking work with the local database, but new discovery requires a connection.
